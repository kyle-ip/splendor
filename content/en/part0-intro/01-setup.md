Correct setup avoids disputes. Adjust gem tokens and noble tiles by player count, then lay out the display.

## Components

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Gem tokens | Emerald, sapphire, ruby, diamond, onyx; plus **gold** wild tokens (5) |
| Development cards | Level 1 ×40, Level 2 ×30, Level 3 ×20; shuffle each level face down |
| Noble tiles | 10 different tiles; reveal **players + 1** |

## Player Count Setup

| Players | Tokens per gem color | Noble tiles |
|---------|----------------------|-------------|
| 2 | 4 each | 3 |
| 3 | 5 each | 4 |
| 4 | 7 each | 5 |

Gold tokens are always **5**, regardless of player count.

## Setup Steps

1. Place tokens by color in the bank (center of the table)
2. Shuffle each level deck and **reveal 4 cards** face up per level
3. Shuffle noble tiles; reveal players + 1 face up; remove the rest
4. Starting player: the **youngest** player begins; play proceeds clockwise (groups may agree otherwise as a house rule)

## Setup Tips

- Display has 12 cards (4 per level), visible to all
- When a card is bought or reserved, refill from that level’s deck
- Once a deck is empty, that level is no longer refilled
