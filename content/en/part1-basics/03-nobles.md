Nobles are an extra source of 3 prestige. When you meet their requirements, they visit **automatically**.

## Trigger Conditions

Each noble tile lists the bonus colors required, for example:

- Emerald ×3, sapphire ×3, onyx ×3

When your **total bonuses** meet the requirements, that noble visits at the end of your turn.

## Important Rules

- Nobles **do not consume** bonuses or gems—they only check your bonus totals
- At most **1 noble** per turn (if you qualify for several, choose one)
- A noble visit gives **+3 prestige** and has no other effect
- Visited nobles are not counted again

## Strategy Tip (Beginner)

At setup, quickly scan noble requirements and note which colors appear often. You don't have to force nobles, but if your path naturally aligns, 3 points is very efficient.

## Common Misconception

> "You must hand over matching gems to attract a noble" — **False**  
> Only bonuses matter; keep your gems for buying cards.
