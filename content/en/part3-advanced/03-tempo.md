**Tempo** is how fast you advance scoring and your engine relative to opponents—are you ahead or behind?

## High Tempo (Accelerate)

- Buy continuously; take gems less often
- Attract nobles early
- Best when: cheap bonus cards are in the display and the noble path is clear

## Low Tempo (Stockpile)

- Take gems toward expensive cards
- Reserve while waiting for better refills
- Best when: 2-player control, or waiting for a key Level 3

## Tempo Switch Signals

**Speed up when**:

- Opponent has ≥12 prestige and you have ≤9
- The display refills a cheap card you need
- A noble is one step away

**Slow down when**:

- Your hand is nearly full with no buy target
- Your only target needs 2 turns of gems
- In 2-player you're ahead by 5+ and can drag their pace

## Balance Principle

Most games: **mid tempo early, high tempo mid-game, burst or trigger late**. Avoid only taking gems all game or only buying high scorers at random.
