Denial is a competitive tool, but it should not **maliciously stall** the game.

## Legal Denial Methods

- Buy a card an opponent urgently needs (best if you can use it too)
- Take key gems from the bank
- Reserve the unique answer
- Reach a noble first

## Don't Overdo It

- Buying expensive cards **only** to block an opponent when they're useless to you
- Multiple turns with no constructive buys
- Creating table disputes over "bad sportsmanship"

## Efficient Denial

Prefer **two birds with one stone**: cards that help your engine or score while hurting an opponent.

## Player Count and Denial

- **2 players**: Highest denial ROI
- **4 players**: Blocking one may help another—be more careful

## Mindset

When denied, stay calm and switch paths. The Splendor display changes every turn—there's often a Plan B.
