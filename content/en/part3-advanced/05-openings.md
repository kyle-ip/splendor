The direction you choose in the first 2–3 turns often sets the skeleton of the whole game.

## Common Paths

### Noble Sprint

- Condition: Noble requirements strongly match the Level 1 display
- Goal: Attract the first noble in 7–9 turns
- Risk: Multiple players contesting the same colors

### Mono-Color Engine → High Points

- Condition: Dense Level 1 cards of one color
- Goal: Quickly reach 3+ bonuses of one color, then chain Level 2/3 buys
- Risk: Nobles may not align

### Hybrid Path (Most Stable)

- First 2–3 bonus cards, then decide noble or points based on refills
- Fits most games, especially 3–4 players

## Sample Lines (T1–T5)

These are **templates**, not scripts—adjust to the actual display.

### Noble sprint (3/3/3 on green–red–black)

| Turn | Action |
|------|--------|
| T1 | Take green + red + black (or buy a free/cheap matching bonus) |
| T2 | Buy cheapest green or red on display |
| T3 | Take missing color(s) or buy the second bonus |
| T4 | Buy third color bonus / gold-bridge reserve if blocked |
| T5 | Buy the last gap or take gems to finish the noble next turn |

### Mono blue engine

| Turn | Action |
|------|--------|
| T1 | Take blue-heavy mix (2 blue if legal and useful) |
| T2–T3 | Buy two cheap +1 blue cards |
| T4 | Take toward a Level 2 blue or flexible 2-pt |
| T5 | Buy Level 2 if net cost is low; else third blue bonus |

### Hybrid open

| Turn | Action |
|------|--------|
| T1 | Take three colors that appear on **any** cheap Level 1 |
| T2 | Buy the cheapest useful bonus |
| T3 | Second cheap bonus in a **different** color |
| T4–T5 | After refill: commit to noble sprint **or** mono depth—don’t stay forever undecided |

## Worked Spot

| Fact | Value |
|------|-------|
| Nobles | Two tiles both need blue×3 |
| Level 1 display | Three cheap blue bonuses, one stray green |
| Players | 3 |

| Choice | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Noble sprint on blue | Fast if you win the race; contested by others |
| Mono green | Weak—only one green card visible |
| Hybrid | Take one blue + one other cheap bonus; reassess after refill |

**Conclusion:** When a color is **crowded on nobles and Level 1**, sprint only if you can win the race; otherwise open hybrid and steal the path later.

## Dynamic Adjustment

Opening paths are **not iron laws**. If turn-3 refills completely miss your plan, switch immediately—don't cling to sunk cost.

## Decision Tree (Simplified)

```
Nobles match display? → Yes → Noble sprint
                     → No → Many L1 of one color? → Yes → Engine path
                                               → No → Hybrid; take 1–2 bonuses first
```
