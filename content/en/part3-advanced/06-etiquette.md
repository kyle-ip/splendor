Competitive skill and good table manners go together.

## In-Person Communication

- Announce actions clearly: "Take 3 different," "Buy this card"
- Call out noble visits and endgame triggers promptly
- You may politely remind someone who is taking long, but respect thinking time

## Rules Disputes

- Gem counts: standard rules keep **counts public**, not the color breakdown of each player's hand
- Reserved cards: you may look at your own reserves anytime
- When disputed, check the rules quick-reference appendix or the official rulebook

## Post-Game Habits

After each game, spend 2 minutes noting (this site's **review template** helps):

- Opening nobles and the path you chose
- Key turning turns
- Mistake types: slow engine / missed noble / endgame math error

## Path to Mastery

Splendor mastery = zero rules mistakes + a stable engine + reading the table + endgame math. Play, review, and use this guide's tools for evaluation and quizzes.
