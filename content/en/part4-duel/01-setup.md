## Components

| Item | Count / note |
|------|----------------|
| Victory tile | 1 (shows the three win conditions) |
| Jewel cards | 67 in 3 levels (Level 1 / 2 / 3 decks) |
| Bag | 1 (holds spent / returned tokens) |
| Privilege scrolls | 3 |
| Tokens | 4 of each gem color (blue, white, green, black, red), **2 Pearls**, **3 Gold** — **25** total |
| Board | 1 (spaces for tokens in a printed spiral) |
| Royal cards | 4 |

## Setup (in order)

1. **Shuffle** the three Jewel decks separately. Stack them in a column with **Level 1 at the bottom**.
2. **Reveal a pyramid** below the Victory tile:
   - **3** Level-3 cards
   - **4** Level-2 cards
   - **5** Level-1 cards
3. Place the **board** below the pyramid. Put all **25 tokens** on the board at random, filling from the **center** outward along the **printed spiral**.
4. Place the **3 Privileges** above the board.
5. Place the **4 Royal cards** below the board (available to claim later).
6. Choose the **first player** at random. The **second player** takes **1 Privilege** and places it in front of themself.

## Table Layout (mental picture)

Top → bottom: Victory tile → card pyramid (L3 / L2 / L1) → token board → Royal cards. Privileges sit above the board. Each player’s purchased cards stay in front of them, sorted by bonus color; reserved cards stay hidden.

## Privilege Reminder (setup)

Privileges are a scarce resource. When the supply above the board is empty and you must take a Privilege, take one **from your opponent**. If you already hold all three, nothing happens.
