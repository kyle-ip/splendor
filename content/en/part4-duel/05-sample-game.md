A short annotated line for **Splendor Duel**. Seats: **You** then **Opponent**. Focus on Privilege gifts, Crown vs race-to-20, and an immediate win—not perfect optimal play.

## Opening Snapshot

Token board has mixed colors and Pearls. You hold 1 Privilege. Face-up threats: a **2-Crown** jewel, a **sapphire 3-pt**, and a Pearl-heavy Level 1.

| Turn | Actor | Play | Why |
|------|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | You | Take a mixed 2-token line (no Privilege gift) | Early greed (3 same / 2 Pearls) would hand Opp a Privilege |
| 1 | Opp | Take toward the 2-Crown jewel | Crowns are a parallel clock from turn one |
| 2 | You | Buy a cheap sapphire bonus | Seeds monocolor **and** discounts |
| 2 | Opp | Reserve the contested sapphire 3-pt (+Gold if available) | Denial + wild insurance |

**Callout:** After T2, watch **Opp Crowns**, your **sapphire stack**, and totals—not only the race to 20.

## Midgame — Privilege and Crowns

| Turn | Actor | Play | Why |
|------|-------|------|-----|
| 3 | You | Replenish only if you need the refill more than Opp needs Privilege | Replenish gifts Privilege—pay for it with a buy next |
| 3 | Opp | Buy the 2-Crown jewel (now at 2 Crowns) | Halfway to the first Royal threshold (3) |
| 4 | You | Use Privilege to snipe one sapphire off the board | Soft denial without a full take |
| 4 | Opp | Take 3 of one color → **you gain a Privilege** | Classic greed tax |
| 5 | You | Buy / reserve toward Crown **or** sapphire points | Decide your primary clock |
| 5 | Opp | Hits **3 Crowns** → claims a Royal (tempo spike) | Treat Royals as spikes, not flavor |

**Fork (you):** If Opp is about to hit **6 Crowns**, deny that path even if it slows your race to 20. If Opp’s Crowns stall, cash sapphire prestige toward same-color 10 or total 20.

## Endgame — Immediate Win

| Turn | Actor | Play | Why |
|------|-------|------|-----|
| 6–7 | Both | Update Crown / color / total every buy | Duel ends **immediately**—no “finish the round” |
| 8 | You | Buy a sapphire card to **≥10 sapphire prestige** | Monocolor win while Opp stares at Crowns |
| — | — | Game over at once | Opp does not get a reply turn |

Alternate finish: you reach **≥20 total** on a sure 3-pt while Opp sits on 5 Crowns—still win immediately. Alternate loss: you chase 20 while Opp quietly buys the 6th Crown and a Royal that pushes them over.

## What to Rehearse

1. Prefer mixed takes early; price every Privilege gift  
2. Track three clocks after every opponent buy  
3. Spoil the line they are clearly on (Crowns vs color vs total)  
4. Remember: first trigger wins—count reserved Crowns and color cards
